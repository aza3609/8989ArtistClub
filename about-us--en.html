8989 Artist Club welcomes writers of any genre. This group, made up of artists living in the 1980s and 1990s in Korean art history, talks about the stark reality that one cannot just immerse themselves in artistic activities. Created by an artist born in 1989, a member of the generation involved, it is a record of artists living in the era of capitalism and was created for solidarity with the purpose of continuation.
